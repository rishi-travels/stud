# **App Name**: Chhaya Bajaj Auto

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Homepage Display: Showcase Chhaya Bajaj's featured vehicles, latest news, and ongoing promotions with responsive carousels and highlight sections.
- Informative About Us Section: Present the agency's history, mission, values, and team members through engaging content blocks and imagery.
- Interactive EMI Calculator: Allow users to input vehicle price, down payment, interest rate, and tenure to instantly calculate estimated monthly installments.
- Comprehensive Contact Page: Provide a submission form for inquiries, along with contact details, location map, and business hours.
- Careers and Opportunities Page: Display available job openings with descriptions, requirements, and an option to submit a basic application of interest.
- Responsive Navigation System: Implement a clear and intuitive menu that adapts seamlessly across all devices, ensuring easy access to all main sections.
- AI Career Pathway Tool: A tool that, based on a user's skills and interests submitted via the careers page, recommends suitable job roles or suggests improvements for their application.

## Style Guidelines:

- The brand aesthetic embodies professionalism and modern automotive elegance. The primary color, a rich and inviting blue (#5282E0), offers vibrancy while maintaining sophistication. The background, a deep desaturated blue-grey (#1B1F27), creates a premium, immersive canvas. A vibrant, cool cyan accent (#75DCF0) is used sparingly for interactive elements and highlights, ensuring excellent contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean lines and excellent readability, suitable for both concise headings and detailed information about vehicles and services.
- Utilize a consistent set of sleek, modern line icons for navigation elements, feature indicators, and contact methods to maintain a clean and professional aesthetic.
- Employ a responsive grid-based layout with generous white space to enhance content readability and visual hierarchy on all device sizes. Content sections should be clearly defined and modular.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions for navigation hovers, image carousels, and section reveals, adding a touch of dynamism without distracting from the core content.
